Making my first Bsky Bot

Date Fri 13 June 2025 By jymmyboi Category misc.

Context

To begin here, this was going to be a twitter bot but I didn't want to pay to post one post a week.

I don't like corruption. I feel like the AFL (Australian Football League) should be a pretty clean operation for things like corruption but I feel like the league is rife with a lot of issues.

One of the biggest issues I have found this year was the fact that a certain umpire by the name of Nick Foot has taken a job at SportsBet and the AFL and AFL Umpires Association has believed that this is just OK. I do not and so do a lot of other people in the community. It just doesn't pass the pub test.

What can I do?

The AFL umpire house create a roster ever week of the appointments for the week. I am not sure exactly when they come out but I know it is by 6pm-7pm Adelaide time. So I created a bot that scrapes the newest rounds appointments and posts when Nick Foot is umpiring.

Is this petty?

Yes.

Did I learn a lot?

Also yes.

What does the bot do?

Basically the flow of the code is as follows

  • Scrapes the HTML of the AFLUA appointments page
  • Finds the link for the PDF of the latest round
  • Downloads said PDF
  • Converts to a txt file
  • Analyses the text for when NF is umpiring
  • Posts it to Bsky
